Part `PX900-SEAL-A2` exists in the spare parts catalog with description "chamber seal kit, fluoroelastomer".

This is a fluoroelastomer-based seal rated for sustained plasma exposure at process temperatures
up to 120°C and fluorine chemistry (including NF₃, CF₄, SF₆ processes). Do not substitute with
standard Buna-N or EPDM seals; they are not rated for fluorine and will fail prematurely, voiding
the chamber warranty.

## Ordering Information

**Standard orders:** Submit via the Acme Customer Portal or email parts@acmefab.com.

**Urgent orders (next-day air available):** Call 1-800-ACM-PART, option 3. Next-day air
surcharge: 25% of parts price, minimum $85. Available for stock items only (see "Lead Time"
column; items with > 7 day lead time are built-to-order and cannot be expedited via next-day air).

**Volume quotes:** For orders exceeding $10,000, contact your account manager for a formal quote.
Volume discounts apply per line item as listed above; combined order discounts are available
by negotiation.

**International orders:** DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) pricing available for orders to the EU,
Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan. Contact export@acmefab.com for a DDP quote.

## Warranty

All Acme-genuine parts carry a 12-month warranty from the date of installation when installed
by a qualified Acme field service engineer or trained customer technician. Warranty void if:
- Installed using non-Acme installation tools (e.g., torque wrench calibration not traceable)
- Exposed to non-Acme process chemistries not listed in the part specification
