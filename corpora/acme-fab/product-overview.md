## 1. Company Overview

Acme Fab Equipment designs and manufactures plasma processing equipment for the global
semiconductor industry. Founded in 1998 and headquartered in San Jose, California, Acme serves
leading-edge logic, memory, MEMS, and compound semiconductor fabs on four continents.

Acme's equipment portfolio focuses on etch and deposition processes for 200 mm and 300 mm
wafer platforms. Our distinguishing capability is process uniformity at scale: Acme tools
consistently achieve < ±2% within-wafer uniformity on production-proven processes.

## 2. PX Series: Plasma Etchers

### PX-900 (Current Generation)

The PX-900 is Acme's flagship 300 mm ICP plasma etcher for leading-edge logic and memory fabs.
It is qualified for sub-5 nm node etch processes including EUV hard mask etch, self-aligned
patterning, and gate dielectric etch.

Key specifications:
- Wafer size: 300 mm
- Plasma source: 13.56 MHz ICP, 200–3000 W
- Bias RF: 400 kHz, 0–1000 W
- Chamber pressure: 1–200 mTorr
- Process temperature: 20–80°C
- Base pressure: < 5 × 10⁻⁷ Torr

Maintenance highlight: The PX-900 chamber seal (PX900-SEAL-A2) requires replacement every
1,200 RF hours. See the Maintenance Manual (PX900-MM-Rev4) for the full PM schedule.

### PX-700 (Legacy, End-of-Life)

The PX-700 is a 200 mm etcher for mature node applications. It entered end-of-life in 2024;
Acme continues to supply parts and Tier 3/4 service contracts until 2029. Customers with
PX-700 installed base are encouraged to contact their account manager about migration paths.

## 3. LT Series: Load Locks

### LT-200

The LT-200 is a single-wafer nitrogen-purged load lock designed for PX-900 cluster tool
configurations. It features a temperature-controlled wafer stage (20–80°C), EFEM-compatible
interface, and automatic pump-down and vent sequences.

Idle standby temperature is set to 45°C by default; see LT200-OM-Rev2 for configuration details.

### LT-100 (Legacy)

The LT-100 is the 200 mm load lock paired with the PX-700. It follows the same end-of-life
schedule as the PX-700.
