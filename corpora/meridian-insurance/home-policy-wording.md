# HomeShield Plus: Policy Wording

**Policy product:** HomeShield Plus
**Issuer:** Meridian Insurance Company Limited
**Document reference:** MI-HSP-PW-Rev8
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026

*This document forms the basis of the insurance contract between you (the policyholder) and
Meridian Insurance. Please read it carefully. Keep it in a safe place with your policy schedule.*

---

## Section 1: Definitions

**1.1** "We", "us", "our" — Meridian Insurance Company Limited.

**1.2** "You", "your" — the policyholder named in the policy schedule.

**1.3** "Insured property" — the home and its permanent fixtures and fittings at the address
shown in your policy schedule.

**1.4** "Insured event" — a sudden, unforeseen, and accidental occurrence causing physical
loss or damage to the insured property or its contents.

**1.5** "Excess" — the first portion of any claim which you must bear yourself, as shown in
your policy schedule or as specified in the relevant section below.

**1.6** "Policy period" — the period of insurance shown in your policy schedule.

**1.7** "Gradual seepage" — water or moisture ingress occurring over a continuous or recurring
period without a discrete, identifiable triggering event.

---

## Section 2: Buildings Cover

**2.1** We will pay for damage to the structure of the insured property (walls, roof, floors,
permanent fixtures) caused by a sudden, accidental, and unforeseeable insured event.

**2.2** Buildings cover includes:

- Fire, lightning, and explosion
- Windstorm (wind speeds exceeding 60 km/h)
- Hail and snow loading
- Earthquake (excess applies per §3.4)
- Impact damage from falling trees or vehicles
- Burst pipes (sudden, see §3.1)
- Riot and malicious damage

---

## Section 3: Water Damage

### 3.1 Covered Water Events

We cover sudden and accidental water damage arising from:
- Bursting, leaking, or overflowing of fixed water supply pipes or tanks within the property
- Overflow of fixed baths, basins, sinks, or showers due to a blockage
- Escape of water from a washing machine, dishwasher, or other domestic appliance

For sudden burst pipe events, the standard excess under HomeShield Plus is **$500** (see §3.2).

### 3.2 Accidental Damage — Standard Excess

The standard excess for accidental damage claims under the HomeShield Plus plan is $500.

This excess applies to all Section 2 and Section 3 claims unless a higher excess is shown in
your policy schedule (some properties carry a rated excess for water damage history) or unless
a specific excess applies under §3.4 or §4.2.1.

### 3.3 Excluded Water Events

We do not cover:

**3.3.1** Water damage arising from a flood (rising water from a natural watercourse). Flood
cover is available as an optional extension; see Section 9.

**3.3.2** Seawater ingress through windows, doors, or roofing.

**3.3.3** Condensation, dampness, or moisture not arising from a discrete insured event.

**3.3.4 Gradual seepage (Clause 4.2.1)**

Clause 4.2.1: Water damage arising from gradual seepage over a period exceeding 14 days is excluded from coverage.

This exclusion applies regardless of whether the seepage was visible or detectable during the
period. "Gradual seepage" includes:

- Water leaking slowly through cracks in walls or foundations over weeks or months
- Roof leaks that have been present but untreated for more than 14 days
- Pipe condensation or drip accumulation building up over time
- Ground moisture rising through floors or basements without a discrete triggering event

*Rationale:* Gradual seepage constitutes a maintenance failure, not an insured event, and
the damage is considered preventable by reasonable property upkeep. The 14-day threshold
aligns with industry standards for distinguishing sudden events from maintenance neglect.

If you are unsure whether your water damage event meets the threshold for coverage, contact
Meridian Claims on 1-800-MER-CLMS before proceeding. A claims assessor can review photographic
evidence and property records to determine whether the damage is sudden or gradual.

### 3.4 Earthquake and Seismic Water Damage

Water damage following an earthquake is covered under the earthquake sub-limit (50% of sum
insured), with a separate excess of $2,500 or 2% of the claimed amount, whichever is higher.
This applies even where the triggering mechanism is burst pipes cracked by seismic activity.

## Section 4: Contents Cover

**4.1** We cover accidental loss or damage to household contents at the insured property,
subject to the sum insured shown in your policy schedule.

**4.2** Contents cover includes personal items, furniture, appliances, clothing, and sporting
equipment. For high-value items, see §7.

---

## Section 5: Liability Cover

**5.1** We provide public liability cover of $2,000,000 for accidental bodily injury or
property damage caused to third parties at the insured property.

**5.2** This cover excludes liability arising from business activities carried out at the
property, or from intentional acts.

---

## Section 6: Temporary Accommodation

If the insured property becomes uninhabitable as a result of an insured event, we will pay
reasonable costs of comparable temporary accommodation for up to 12 months or until the
property is repaired, whichever occurs first. Maximum benefit: $75,000.

---

## Section 7: High-Value Items

**7.1** Contents cover includes items up to $2,500 per item. Items valued above this require
specific listing on the policy schedule.

**7.2** For jewellery, watches, and other portable valuables:

Jewellery items valued above $5,000 must be individually listed on the policy schedule.

Failure to individually list a jewellery item valued above $5,000 means that item will be
treated as unspecified contents and the $2,500 per-item limit will apply, regardless of actual
value. We recommend having high-value items independently appraised and providing a current
appraisal certificate at policy inception.

**7.3** Other portable valuables (cameras, musical instruments, art) valued above $2,500 must
also be individually listed. The per-item limit for unlisted portable valuables is $2,500.

**7.3.2** Unlisted jewellery or valuables above the applicable threshold are covered only up
to the general per-item limit. Claims for unlisted jewellery above $5,000 will be settled at
$2,500 maximum (see also §7.2).

---

## Section 8: Claims

For claims procedures, see the Meridian Claims Process FAQ. In general:

**8.1** Report insured events promptly. Contact Meridian Claims at 1-800-MER-CLMS as soon as
practicable after the event.

**8.2** Take reasonable steps to prevent further damage (e.g., turn off water supply after a
burst pipe; cover broken windows against weather).

**8.3** Do not authorise or carry out permanent repairs before an assessor has inspected the
damage, unless delay would cause additional harm.

For the claim lodgement deadline, see the Claims Process FAQ.

---

## Section 9: Optional Extensions

The following extensions can be added to HomeShield Plus for an additional premium:

- **Flood extension:** Covers rising water damage; requires flood risk assessment if the
  property is within 100 metres of a watercourse.
- **Accidental damage extension:** Broadens accidental damage beyond standard covered events.
- **Home office extension:** Covers business equipment up to $5,000.
- **Pet liability extension:** Covers liability for damage caused by domestic pets.

---

## Section 10: General Conditions

**10.1 Duty of disclosure:** You must disclose all material facts at inception and renewal.
Failure to disclose may give us the right to avoid the policy or reduce the claim.

**10.2 Fraud:** Any fraudulent claim voids the entire policy.

**10.3 Subrogation:** After we pay a claim, we may pursue recovery from a third party in
your name.

**10.4 Governing law:** This policy is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction shown in your
policy schedule.
