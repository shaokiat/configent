# HomeShield Plus: Product Disclosure Statement

**Document:** MI-HSP-PDS-Rev5
**Issuer:** Meridian Insurance Company Limited
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026

*Read this PDS carefully. It is designed to help you understand what this product covers and
what is excluded, so you can decide whether it is right for you.*

---

## About This Product

HomeShield Plus is a home and contents insurance product offered by Meridian Insurance Company
Limited. It provides cover for the buildings structure, household contents, personal liability,
and temporary accommodation following an insured event at your home.

---

## What We Cover

**Buildings:** Sudden, accidental damage to the structure of your home, including walls, roof,
floors, and permanent fixtures. Covered events include fire, storm, burst pipes, earthquake,
and malicious damage.

**Contents:** Loss or damage to household items, furniture, appliances, clothing, and personal
belongings caused by covered events (fire, theft, accidental damage, water damage from covered
sources).

**Liability:** Up to $2,000,000 for accidental injury or damage to others at your property.

**Temporary accommodation:** Up to $75,000 for comparable accommodation if your home is
uninhabitable after an insured event.

---

## What We Don't Cover (Key Exclusions)

This is a summary. The full exclusions are in the policy wording (MI-HSP-PW-Rev8) and the
Coverage Exclusions List.

- **Flood:** Not covered unless you purchase the optional flood extension.
- **Gradual seepage:** Damage from water leaking or seeping gradually over more than 14 days
  is not an insured event (Clause 4.2.1 of the policy wording).
- **Wear and tear, rust, rot:** These are maintenance issues, not sudden events.
- **Unlisted high-value jewellery:** Jewellery worth more than $5,000 per item must be
  individually listed or the per-item limit of $2,500 applies.
- **Business activities:** Property or liability arising from running a business from home
  (unless a home office extension is purchased).

---

## Significant Features

- **Claims within 30 days:** You must report claims within 30 days of the event (or discovery
  of damage) unless exceptional circumstances prevent this.
- **Standard excess:** For accidental damage claims under HomeShield Plus, the standard excess
  is $500. Higher or lower excesses may apply depending on your policy schedule or the type
  of event.
- **Coastal and flood-risk properties:** Properties within 100 metres of a coastline require a
  flood risk assessment before the flood extension can be added.

---

## Optional Extensions

You can enhance your cover by adding:
- Flood extension (requires flood risk assessment for coastal/riparian properties)
- Accidental damage extension (broader accidental cover for contents)
- Home office extension (covers business equipment up to $5,000)
- Portable valuables extension (covers specified items away from the home)
- Pet liability extension

---

## Excesses

| Event Type | Standard Excess |
|-----------|----------------|
| Accidental damage (all sections) | $500 |
| Earthquake | $2,500 or 2% of claim (whichever is higher) |
| Theft with no forced entry | $750 |
| Glass breakage (buildings) | $250 |

Higher excesses may be shown on your individual policy schedule.
