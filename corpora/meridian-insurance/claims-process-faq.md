# Claims Process FAQ

**Document:** MI-Claims-FAQ-Rev6
**Issuer:** Meridian Insurance Company Limited
**Revision date:** March 2026

This FAQ covers the claims process for HomeShield Plus and other personal lines products.
For commercial lines claims, contact your broker.

---

## Making a Claim

### Q: How do I report a claim?

Contact Meridian Claims by:

- **Phone:** 1-800-MER-CLMS (24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergencies)
- **Online:** claims.meridianinsurance.com (business hours portal; emergency incidents should
  use the phone line)
- **App:** The Meridian app allows photo uploads and claim tracking

Have your policy number, the date of the event, and a description of the damage ready.

### Q: How quickly must I report a claim?

Claims must be lodged within 30 days of the insured event unless exceptional circumstances apply.

"Lodging" a claim means formally registering it with Meridian Claims (by phone, online, or
app), not necessarily completing the full claims documentation. If you discover damage later
(for example, after returning from a holiday), the 30-day period runs from the date you
discovered or reasonably should have discovered the damage.

"Exceptional circumstances" include hospitalisation, natural disasters affecting communication
systems, or other documented reasons why it was not reasonably practicable to lodge within
30 days. Contact the claims team as soon as possible in these situations.

If you lodge a claim after 30 days without exceptional circumstances, we may decline the
claim or apply a late-lodgement reduction to the settlement.

### Q: Do I need to provide evidence of the damage?

Yes. You will need to provide:

- Photographs of the damage (taken as soon as safely possible after the event)
- A list of damaged or destroyed items with estimated values
- For contents claims: proof of ownership (receipts, bank statements, photographs)
- For high-value items (jewellery, art, electronics): appraisal certificates or purchase
  records where available
- For theft: a police report or crime reference number

## The Claims Process

### Q: What happens after I lodge a claim?

1. **Acknowledgement:** Meridian will acknowledge receipt within 2 business days.
2. **Assessment:** A claims assessor will contact you within 5 business days to arrange an
   inspection (if required) or to request documentation.
3. **Decision:** We aim to make a coverage decision within 10 business days of receiving all
   required documentation. Complex or disputed claims may take longer.
4. **Settlement:** Once the claim is accepted, we will either arrange for repairs through one
   of our preferred contractors or settle by cash payment.

### Q: Can I use my own contractor for repairs?

For claims under $2,500, you may arrange your own repairs and claim reimbursement. For claims
over $2,500, Meridian will appoint a preferred contractor unless you request otherwise in
writing. Using a preferred contractor does not affect the settlement amount.

### Q: What is the excess and when do I pay it?

The excess is the first portion of the claim you must bear. It is deducted from the settlement
amount, not paid upfront. Under HomeShield Plus, the standard excess for accidental damage is
$500. Different excesses may apply for other event types (see the policy wording, Section 3
and Section 4). The excess is shown on your policy schedule.

### Q: My ceiling has been leaking for several weeks. Will it be covered?

This depends on whether the leak is "gradual seepage" as defined in Clause 4.2.1 of the
HomeShield Plus policy wording (Section 3.3.4). If the leak has been developing gradually
over more than 14 days, it will generally not be covered. If a sudden event (e.g., a burst
pipe or storm damage) caused the leak, it may be covered even if you did not notice it
immediately, provided the damage is recent.

Meridian's assessor will consider photographic evidence, the property's maintenance history,
and any available plumber's reports to determine the nature of the event. If in doubt, contact
us before carrying out repairs.

## Disputes and Complaints

### Q: I disagree with the claim decision. What can I do?

1. **Internal review:** Contact the Claims Manager and request a written explanation of the
   decision. Provide any additional evidence you wish to have considered.
2. **Complaints:** If you remain dissatisfied, lodge a formal complaint via
   complaints.meridianinsurance.com. Meridian will respond within 15 business days.
3. **External review:** If Meridian's final response does not resolve the dispute, you may
   refer the matter to the Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS) or the relevant state insurance
   commissioner.

### Q: Can I claim for temporary repairs I did to prevent further damage?

Yes. Reasonable emergency or temporary repairs to prevent further damage are generally
reimbursable, even if carried out before the assessor visits. Keep receipts for all materials
and labour. Do not carry out permanent repairs without prior approval from Meridian.
