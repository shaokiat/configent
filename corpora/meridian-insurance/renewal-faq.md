## Renewals

### Q: How does my policy renew?

HomeShield Plus renews automatically each year unless you cancel before the renewal date.
Meridian will send a renewal notice at least 21 days before expiry. The notice includes:

- Your renewed premium
- Any changes to terms and conditions
- Your no-claims discount (if applicable)
- A reminder to update your sum insured

### Q: My premium has increased. Why?

Premium changes at renewal reflect:

- Changes in the risk profile of your property or neighbourhood (e.g., increased claims in
  your postcode)
- Changes in Meridian's overall claims experience and reinsurance costs
- Inflation in building and contents replacement costs
- Your personal claims history (a claim in the past year may remove a no-claims discount)

If you have a legitimate query about a large increase, contact Meridian Customer Service.

### Q: Can I negotiate my renewal premium?

Contact Meridian at least 14 days before renewal if you wish to discuss your premium.
Meridian may be able to offer a retention discount, adjust your sum insured, or discuss
excess options that can lower your premium.

### Q: What happens if I don't pay the renewal premium?

If the renewal premium is not received before the expiry date, your policy will lapse.
A lapsed policy provides no cover. Meridian will attempt to contact you via email and post
before the policy lapses.

## Policy Changes

### Q: I've renovated my home. Do I need to update my policy?

Yes. If renovations have increased the rebuild cost of your home, you must update your
buildings sum insured. Significant structural changes (extensions, garage additions,
swimming pools) must be notified to Meridian.

Failure to disclose major structural changes may constitute a breach of your duty of
disclosure, potentially affecting your ability to claim.

### Q: I've acquired expensive new jewellery. What should I do?

For any jewellery item valued over $5,000, you must notify Meridian and have it added as a
specified item on the policy schedule. Jewellery items valued above $5,000 must be
individually listed on the policy schedule — failure to list them means claims will be settled
at the per-item contents limit of $2,500, not the full value.

Contact Meridian with an appraisal certificate, and we will add the item to your schedule with
a small additional premium.

### Q: I'm moving house. Can I transfer my policy?

Yes. Notify Meridian at least 5 business days before moving. Meridian will re-rate the policy
for the new property. If the new property has different risk characteristics (e.g., coastal
location requiring a flood risk assessment), additional underwriting may be required before
transfer is confirmed.

## Cancellations

### Q: How do I cancel my policy?

Contact Meridian Customer Service by phone or in writing. If cancelling mid-term:

- **At your request:** A pro-rata refund of the unexpired premium applies, less a $45
  cancellation administration fee. No refund applies if a claim has been made in the current
  period.
- **At Meridian's request:** 21 days' written notice; full pro-rata refund.

### Q: Can Meridian cancel my policy?

Yes, in limited circumstances: fraud or misrepresentation, failure to pay premium, or a change
in risk profile outside Meridian's appetite. Meridian must provide 21 days' notice.
